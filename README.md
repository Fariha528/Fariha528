About

I'm a Computer Science (AI & Data Science) student at MIT World Peace University, building predictive models and full-stack AI systems that go beyond notebooks — from a real estate valuation engine deployed with an end-to-end ML pipeline, to a hybrid Retrieval-Augmented Generation system combining knowledge graphs, FAISS, and LLMs for healthcare consultation.

My work sits at the intersection of applied machine learning, product engineering, and research — I care as much about model performance (R² of 0.9597 on my last production model) as I do about shipping something a user can actually touch, via FastAPI backends and React frontends. I've co-authored an IEEE publication on IoT-enabled battery management and have a second paper accepted at MVAI 2026 on Ayurvedic NLP systems.

Currently sharpening backend architecture and deployment practices while continuing to build systems that turn unstructured data into explainable, real-time decisions.
